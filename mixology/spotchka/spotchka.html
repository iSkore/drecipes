    # Spotchka

    #

    # ingredients:

    • 1.5 floz gin or vodka
    • 0.5 floz blue curacao
    • 0.33 floz lime juice
    • 1 pinch of purple or blue Luster Dust (opens new window) for cloudiness
    • splash of simple syrup
    • shake vigorously over ice, then strain into the glass
    • top with club soda to taste (about 3 oz)
