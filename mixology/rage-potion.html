# rage potion

  • 1 floz lemon juice
  • 0.5 floz simple syrup
  • 2 floz vodka
  • 0.25 floz raspberry liqueur
  • 0.15 floz blue curacao or blueberry liqueur
  • 1 barspoon of yellow chartreuse
  • shake and strain into potion bottle
  • top with seltzer
