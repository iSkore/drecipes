    # potato casserole (margaret)

    # ingredients:

    • 3 Bopes [?] of hash brown potatoes
    • 1 qt qt [? quart] diced potatoes
    • 1 cup sour cream
    • 0.5 cup chopped onion
    • 1 cup cream chicken soup
    • 2 cups shredded sharp cheese

    Topping

    • 0.25 cup melted butter
    • 2 cups crushed corn flakes

    # instructions:

    • mix all of first part together and salt and pepper to taste
    • bake 45 minutes at 350º
    • add topping the last 15 minutes
