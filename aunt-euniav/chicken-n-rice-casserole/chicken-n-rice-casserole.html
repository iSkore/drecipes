    # chicken 'n rice casserole

    light and zesty

    ready to bake in just 7 minutes

    # ingredients:

    • 4 chicken breast halves
    • 0.33 cups bottled italian salad dressing
    • 0.66 cups uncooked regular rice
    • 1 bag (160oz) frozen broccoli, carrots, water chestnuts, red pepper combination
    • 1 can (8oz) durkee's french fried onion
    • 1.75 cups chicken bouillon
    • 0.5 tsp durkee italian seasoning

    # instructions:

    • place chicken breast in baking dish
    • pour salad dressing over chicken
    • bake uncovered at 400º for 20 minutes
    • place rice, vegetables and can durkee onions around and under chicken
    • combine bouillon and italian seasoning
    • pour over chicken and vegetables
    • bake uncovered [?] 25 minutes
    • top with remaining onions
    • bake 2-3 minutes longer
    • let stand 5 minutes before serving
    • makes 4 servings
