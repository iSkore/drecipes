# Apple Butter

  • 12 cups apples
  • 12 cups sugar (5 lbs)
  • 1 box regular strawberry jello
  • 1 tsp cinnomon oil (2 tsp)
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • stir all together
  • cook 20 minutes thick (15 minutes)
