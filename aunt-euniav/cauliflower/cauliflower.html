    # cauliflower

    # ingredients:

    • 1 whole cauliflower (1 lbs)
    • 0.5 cup mayonnaise
    • 1 tsp mustard
    • 1 tsp minced onion
    • 0.5 cup cheese [?]

    # instructions:

    • cut off bottom part but leave together
    • cook until done
    • take out of pot, place in baking dish
    • mix cup mayonnaise, 1 tsp mustard, 1 tsp minced onion
    • spread over cauliflower and sprinkle cheese over top and place in oven till cheese melts
