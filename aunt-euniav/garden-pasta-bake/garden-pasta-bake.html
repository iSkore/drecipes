    # garden pasta bake

    prep time: 10 minutes cook time: 25 minutes servings: 8

    # ingredients:

    • 170 g penne pasta
    • 0.5 cup chopped onion
    • 1 tbsp butter
    • 1 can (14.5 oz) del monte freshcut diced tomatoes with basil, garlic & oregano
    • 1 can (15.25 oz) del monte freshcut golden sweet whole kernel corn, drained
    • 1 can (14.5 oz) del monte freshcut cut green beans, drained
    • 1 can condensed (10.75 oz) cream of mushroom soup
    • 2 can shredded monterey jack cheese
    • (variation) for a heartier dish, add 2 cups diced cooked ham or chicken

    # instructions:

    • cook pasta as package directs; drain
    • cook onion in butter in 10-inch skillet until tender
    • add tomatoes, corn, green beans, and of cheese
    • stir in soup; mix well
    • combine pasta with vegetable mixture
    • season to taste with salt and pepper, if desired
    • pour into inch baking dish or casserole dish
    • top with remaining cheese
    • bake at 350ºF, 20 minutes or until hot
