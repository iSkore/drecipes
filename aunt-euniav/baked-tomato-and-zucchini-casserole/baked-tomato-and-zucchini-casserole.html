    # baked tomato and zucchini casserole

    # ingredients:

    • 3 fresh zucchini
    • 6 small onions sliced
    • 0.33 cup chopped parsley
    • 1 tsp dried basil
    • 5 small ripe tomatoes sliced
    • 0.25 cup olive oil
    • salt to taste
    • ground pepper to taste

    # instructions:

    • wash the zucchini and trim both ends
    • cook in boiling water 5-10 minutes and drain
    • slice inch thick and place in a shallow casserole
    • add the onion and of the parsley and season with salt and basil
    • cover with tomatoes and add remaining parsley, pepper and olive oil
    • bake at 350º for 30 to 40 minutes or until vegetables are tender
    • 4 servings
