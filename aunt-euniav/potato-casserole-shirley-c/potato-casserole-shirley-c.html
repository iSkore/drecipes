    # potato casserole (shirley c)

    # ingredients:

    • 2 lbs [?] frozen hash browns (thawed)
    • 1 cup fried onion
    • 1 can cream of chicken soup
    • 454 g sour cream
    • 0.5 stick melted margarine (grease pan with part of this)
    • 227 g grated cheese (sharp)
    • desired salt and pepper to taste

    # instructions:

    • mix all ingredients and bake approximately 1 hour at 350º
    • use pan
