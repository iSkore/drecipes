    # summer squash casserole

    # ingredients:

    • 1 lbs summer squash
    • 0.33 cup crumbled saltines
    • 1 onion chopped
    • 2 tbsp melted butter
    • 0.5 cup finely diced celery
    • 0.5 cup grated cheddar cheese
    • 3 tbsp chopped green chili pepper
    • 2 eggs lightly beaten
    • 1.5 cup milk
    • 1.25 tsp salt
    • dash of pepper

    # instructions:

    • cook the squash in boiling water until tender
    • drain and mash
    • add remaining ingredients and mix well
    • pour into a greased baking dish
    • bake at 375º for 25 minutes
    • 6 servings
